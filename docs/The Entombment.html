Technical Study - The Entombment

  1. Further Examples

This extension uses the IIIF image viewer Mirador to produce a customised presentation of a bespoke selection of IIIF manifests[1]. It is an example of a special page that can be added presented using the simple-site project.

For more information about the simple-site project and details about how this example work please see the simple-site webpages.

Summary

This example presents a series of images take of The Entombment by Rembrandt van Rijn (1606â€“1669), Hunterian Art Gallery, University of Glasgow. These images where taken at the The National Gallery as part of a detailed technical examination. As the painting is relatively small is was possible to take some very high resolution images, with the overall visible image captured at about 1500 ppi and the two visible detail images captured at about 4000 ppi.
